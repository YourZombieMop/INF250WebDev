Featured Projects & Stories

Our team has connected with so many amazing people, we thought it would be fun to share some of the stories of businesses which we've helped. Below you can find short blogs which show what some of our past customers were looking for and how we were able to meet our promise of giving them the opportunity to fulfill their businesses' potential.

Jacob's PC Emporium

Jacob builds custom gaming computers and sells them to people. He has an astounding technical knowledge of hardware, but his website looked dated and he was running out of customers in his area.

We prepared a brand redesign and marketing plan for him. We mentored his sales team to target audiences on the web and helped him set up an e-commerce site. Now, his business fills orders for clients across the country!

Astelle's Family Bakery

Astelle's Family Bakery has been locally-owned and operated for over 20 years. They have served their community with intense passion. However, sadly you weren't be able to see that from their outdated website.

We brought their style to the web to build their local presence and increase their reach in their community. We also brought their business to the top of digital advertising providers with Google Adservices.

Christine's Crazy Mechanisms

Christine develops custom high-tier hardware for car enthusiasts. She was having trouble finding work before she reached out to us. Now, she can barely keep up with her demand!

Aaron's Canned Carwash

Aaron may have canned his carwash business due to feeling overworked, but before he did that, I was able to ramp up his customer base and gave him his hardest work week in the lifetime of his business's operation!
