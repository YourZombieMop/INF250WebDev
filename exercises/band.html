My Favorite Band: Pierce The Veil

Members

  • Vic Fuentes - lead vocals, rhythm guitar, keyboards, piano (2006-present); lead guitar, bass (2006-2007)
  • Tony Perry - lead guitar (2007-present)
  • Jaime Preciado - bass, backing vocals (2007-present)
  • Mike Fuentes - drums (2006-2017); programming (2006-2007)

Favorite 3 Songs

  1. Hold On Till May

    This song is about a partner with a planned suicide date in May due to their trauma. The singer tried to do everything they could to support their partner, but their attempts to help were deflected by the partner and disregarded as attempts to hurt them. The song is a reflection after the suicide which expresses the pain that the singer felt at the realization that their partner chose to let go of the intense love which they shared in favor of death.

  2. I'm Low On Gas and You Need a Jacket

    This song is about someone who keeps doing things which are harmful, but rather than accepting fault and addressing the issues, they make excuses. The singer is running out of energy to support this person, and the song is a reflection upon their relationship as it dies.

  3. The Sky Under the Sea

    This song is about a love interest who uses the singer for their selfish desires while the singer is deeply in love with the person. The singer feels that they would do anything to keep their love interest happy, even at the expense of their own health.
